Folivora Energy is a spin-off of a research institute that conducts research in the fields of control engineering and systems theory.

Particularly for complex energy systems, such as those often found in large buildings, industrial sites, data centers or large infrastructure facilities , mathematical and quantitative methods are necessary in order to obtain the optimum configuration and dimensioning of the components during the planning phase and to maintain the most efficient operation during the operation phase. Operational decisions are often associated with strong dependencies, for example between the energy supply system, production, logistics or other operational processes.
